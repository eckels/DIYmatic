How to make an Origami Iris Flower

By: Evan Eckels

Materials Needed:

  • 1 square piece of Origami Paper

Step 1

First, take your origami paper and fold it into 4 cross sections as shown in the image below and then unfold.

Step 2

Next take the flat sides on two opposite edges and fold themy inside, towards the center bottom flap to make the triangle as seen below.

Step 3

With the four flaps you just created, take the sides of the flap and fold it inwards towards the center of the paper and crease as shown in the picture. Repeat for all four sides.

Step 4

Next, take the top of the flaps and fold them into the center as shown, but do not crease. Then unfold the flaps and repeat on all four sides.

Step 5

Open up the flap on one of the flaps and fold the outsides of the edges of the flap into the center as shown, making sure that the edges line up with the inside of the flower. Repeat on all four sides.

Step 6

Fold the flaps down to the bottom of the flower with the more narrow base. Make sure to crease and then fold the flaps back up to the top. Repeat for each side.

Step 7

Next, fold the bottom of each petal to the middle of the narrow base. Repeat for all four sides.

Step 8

Now fold all the flaps down, these will be your petals.

Step 9

Unfold your flower and then you are finished! You can curl the petals to look like an Iris by wrapping each petal around your finger and holding for a few seconds.
